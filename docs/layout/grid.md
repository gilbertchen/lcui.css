# Grid system

Use our simple grid to build layouts of some sizes thanks to a twelve column system, Sass variables and mixins, and predefined classes.

## Examples

``` grid-demo-xml
<w class="container">
  <w class="row">
    <w class="col col-6" type="textview">
      1 of 2
    </w>
    <w class="col col-6" type="textview">
      2 of 2
    </w>
  </w>
  <w class="row">
    <w class="col col-4" type="textview">
      1 of 3
    </w>
    <w class="col col-4" type="textview">
      2 of 3
    </w>
    <w class="col col-4" type="textview">
      3 of 3

  </w>
  <w class="row">
    <w class="col col-3" type="textview">

    </w>
    <w class="col col-3" type="textview">
      3 of 4
    </w>
    <w class="col col-3" type="textview">
      4 of 4
    </w>
  </w>

    <w class="col col-2" type="textview">
```
